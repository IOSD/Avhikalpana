UNIVERSITY




We aim at clearing all their doubts regarding college and their life thereafter. From giving academic support to letting them develop placement skils, there is a lot in between. We build a platfrom for them to share their grievances with like minded people and together tackling the problem each of the are facing. This provide them emotional and social strenght. they can also mentor students form school . We conduct workshops for students to gain skills required in the real world.

It has been a common experience for college students to get confused among so many career options. They need to know more about every field and then choose for themselves. This can be made possible only by regular interactions with people having similar interests and people currently following similar career option.

Pursuing higher education may help you to become more sensitive to cultural differences and be able to respect the beliefs of all types of people. After completing a college degree, you'll have a broader set of career options, which often leads to increased personal choice and freedom. You could even be healthier as a college graduate, as well.Avhikalpana functions like a link to bridge the gap between those people and mutual benefits.
